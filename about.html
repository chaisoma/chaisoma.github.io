About Buddha Finder

Mission Statement

Buddhism is a way that has aided many, me included, in their fight against mental health issues. However, finding relavent information about issues that you are dealing with can be challenging. Often being met with 40 minute long seminars, rather than short and to the point information. We seek out to provide one space where all the best and easiest to understand resources are availible for viewing, to make finding your Buddha as easy as possible.
